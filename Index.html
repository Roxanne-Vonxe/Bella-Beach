Nestled on an island oasis where the pelicans perch, the blue herons stroll and sugar white sand and emerald waters are so captivating you won’t want to leave. Experience the family-friendly, personal service that makes ourBella Beach hotel a luxury resort vacation destination where generations of families return year after year. Situated directlyon the Gulf of Florida in 6 Wellington St., Melbourne, Bella Beach Resort is exclusively located near Wellignton Point, locally known as “The Pass,” where the Intracoastal Waterway meets the deep waters of the Gulf, and boaters take a voyage for their afternoon venture.
